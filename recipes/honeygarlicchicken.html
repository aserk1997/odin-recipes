Honey Garlic Chicken Recipe

What exactly is this meal?

In a nutshell, if you like sweet but salty at the same time, this is the meal for you!

Now, here's what you need

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Honey
  • Garlic
  • Soy Sauce
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Chop the Chicken Breasts
  2. Add Garlic, Soy Sauce, and Salt to the chopped Chicken
  3. Cook on the stove for about 10 mins
  4. Add the honey once the chicken is golden brown and stir for another 2 mins
  5. Serve and enjoy!
